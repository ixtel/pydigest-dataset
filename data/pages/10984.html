wagtail-simple-demo 0.1.1

Simple demo of Wagtail CMS

To get started, run the following commands in a terminal:

$ pip install wagtail-simple-demo
$ wagtaildemo

After a short moment, the Wagtail demo should start. Visit localhost:8000 in your browser to see the demo in action!

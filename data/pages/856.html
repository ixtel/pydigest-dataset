Notifications

Receive an automatic notification everytime someone mentions your @name, quotes you or answers to your conversation.

Private Conversations

Create your own private conversations and invite as many users as you want to it.

Bookmarks

Spirit automatically registers which was the last comment you read on a conversation.

Archive

Spirit saves all edits made to your comments.

Drafts

Spirit automatically saves your unfinished comments so you can complete them later.

Likes

Highlight any comment you want with a "like."

Responsive

Spirit is mobile-first, it's meant to work in the same way in any given device.

Python & Django

The code has been written on python employing django's framework and it can be easily integrated to pre-existing projects.

Open Source

Spirit is part of the open source initiative, and it's a project released under the terms of the MIT license.

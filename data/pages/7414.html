Today we’re excited to release Anaconda 2.4, which includes Python 3.5 installers, NumPy 1.10, and much more. 

The launch of Anaconda 2.4 brings with it many new features and bug fixes, but it also brings over the new look of Anaconda into the installer - many of you have seen our new logo, and we are happy to have fully integrated the new look and feel of Anaconda into the installation process.

In addition, here are some highlights of Anaconda 2.4:

  • Python 3.5 Support
  • NumPy 1.10
  • OpenBLAS support on Linux
  • Drastic speed improvements to conda
  • Moved from IPython to Jupyter
  • Improved the Start menu on Windows
  • Updated Qt to 4.8.7. on all platforms
  • Updates to more than 60 other packages

Visit our changelog for a full list of changes and updates to Anaconda in version 2.4. To update your version of Anaconda, use

conda update conda
conda install anaconda=2.4

To download Anaconda, please visit our downloads page.

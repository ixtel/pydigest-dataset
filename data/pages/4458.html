loki

PyQt based frontend for locate tool.

Initial version created in a stretch of 6 hours on Tue, 05 May 2015 from 2 to 8 PM.

Hotkeys

Ctrl + F - Focus search bar

Ctrl + L - Clear all items

Ctrl + Q - Quit application

How can I start using your products?

Just click Apply above and we’ll guide you through the process. If you have your ISIC card with you or can access your student mailbox, it will take only a few minutes. Make sure to read the License Terms carefully.

I do not have an ISIC card or student email. What should I do?

At the moment, those are the only two ways we can verify your student status. We do recommend that you get an ISIC card as it offers other bonuses as well.

I already have an Academic License. Do I need to re-apply?

Well, your Academic License is perpetual and you may use it as long as you comply with the license terms in your license agreement. But to get the latest version, you either need to upgrade to a Personal license or apply for a Student License. Note that the Student License covers many more products, and always the latest versions.

Can I use my license on computers in classes?

Actually, for classes we offer a special Classroom License.

Is a free Student License perpetual?

No, the Student License is valid for one year and perpetual licenses are not provided. After one year you will need to renew your Student License for the next year, or purchase a license if you’re no longer a student.

How can I renew my free Student License for another year?

If you’re still a student, feel free to renew the Student License for another year. To do so, use the dedicated link in your JetBrains account, or check the email reminder we send out one week before your license expires for the same link.

Are there any special offers for students upon graduation?

Sure! JetBrains is happy to compliment you on your graduation by providing a 25% Graduation discount, which can be used toward the purchase of a new personal subscription for any single JetBrains product or for the All Products pack. A Graduation discount should be redeemed within 1 year of your Student License expiration date. Use your JetBrains account to purchase a license, and the discount will be applied automatically. Please note that the offer cannot be combined with the Student License renewal. (However, should you renew your Student License, a similar Graduation offer will be available to you the next year.)

What versions of the products do I get?

You always use the latest and the greatest! You can upgrade to any product version released during your license validity period (1 year).

Why aren’t TeamCity and YouTrack included?

Both TeamCity and YouTrack offer free versions/editions that should meet most educational requirements. Just download and use them.

I’m an instructor (teaching staff member). Do I qualify for a free license?

Yes, you can apply for a free license using the same form. We hope you do not mind that we’ll call you a 'student' for now. Also please check out Classroom Licenses that we offer for using our products in classes.

We are an educational institution. Are Student Licenses the right option for us?

Student Licenses are just for students and teachers. Our existing Classroom License allows faculty members to use our products for free, in your classrooms for teaching purposes. There will be no changes to this program and we encourage you to apply if this is your case. Alternatively please contact our sales team and we’ll be glad to discuss special conditions on which we can offer you commercial product licenses.

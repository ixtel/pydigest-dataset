Announcing DjangoVillage 2015

Posted by Massimiliano Pippi and Russell Keith-Magee on February 4, 2015

DjangoVillage is the Italian Django Community Conference and the second edition will be held in Florence, on the 17th to 19th of April during PyCon Sei, the Italian Python Conference.

For the first time, all the Italian Pythoners will round up in one single event: PyCon Sei. Proud of our own peculiarities and origins, DjangoVillage will use the conference to give voice to our projects, to our expectations for the future and to the innovation that we are creating as a community.

The Call for Proposals is now online. DjangoVillage and PyCon are looking for new and old talented speakers. If you love Python and Django, you are passionate about them and you can’t wait to share this with the community, don’t hesitate - submit your proposal now!

The Call for Proposal closes on 9th of February.

This year, the purchase of the ticket will also give you the chance to book an accommodation at a discounted price.

We look forward to seeing you in Florence this April!

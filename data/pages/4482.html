Mommy

Tweet one word per hour describing your mommy, with love <3

Description

On this day, 05/10/2015, is celebrated Mother's Day. Try to describe your mother in 24 words and use the mommy to tweet during this day.

How to run

Edit mommy.py.

  • Put your credentials of twitter (link's in code).
  • Add 24 words on words_mommy list.
  • Write a status to tweet, with one of words_mommy
  • Run ! python mommy.py

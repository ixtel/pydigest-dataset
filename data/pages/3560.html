Welcome to our full-time Django Fellow

Posted by Russell Keith-Magee on March 6, 2015

About a month ago, the Django Software Foundation sent out a call for applicants for a full-time, ongoing Django Fellow. Today, we're proud to announce the successful applicant: Tim Graham.

Tim is already well known to the Django community. He's been a member of the core team for several years, and for the last 18 months has been a major contributor, reviewing and merging pull requests. He was also one of the Django Fellows during the fellowship pilot program.

The Django Fellowship is funded through the generous contributions of donors like you! If you or your company would like to contribute to the ongoing success of the Fellowship program, please visit our donations page. If you'd like to make a larger contribution, or your company is interested in becoming a corporate member of the Django Software Foundation, please get in touch.

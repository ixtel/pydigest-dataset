Free Coke

Automatically generate as many free Coke Zero coupons as required from the offer here, it works with Gmail addresses as they ignore periods in email addresses, for example google@gmail.com is the same as g.o.o.g.l.e@gmail.com to Gmail, therefore we can use this to bypass duplicate email filters. This small script does exactly that by firing off random combinations until a predetermined number of coupons have been generated.

Limitations:

  • Requires a Gmail account, or any other email where periods in emails don't matter.
  • UK only offer.

For our February meetup, Andy Terrel will give a presentation entitled "Building a startup with the PyData Ecosystem", focusing on his work as CTO of Fashion Metric.  Fashion Metric helps online apparel retail sites fit their customers better and they do it with Python.  In this talk, Andy will outline the steps to building out a technology stack with the PyData ecosystem -- things they tried, both successful and otherwise. Audience participation will be encouraged!

Afterwards, we will have time for additional lightning talks, so please get in touch or leave a comment on the Meetup.com page if you'd like to give a short 5-10 minute talk.

Please note that our meetup this month will be back at the Capital Factory, and NOT at the Atlassian office!

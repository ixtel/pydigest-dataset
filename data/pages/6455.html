The purpose of this next section is to look at how players performed last year. The purpose is two fold.

1) By looking at a full season's performance, its good to get a better understanding of a players consistency or lack there of. Typically ESPN and other website just show how a player did overall. This can be tricky because if a player has a two bad weeks, you may bench him and miss his good week.

2 ) Sometimes younger players tend to break out in the second half of the season. I couldn't find a resource that would show fantasy points from game 8 onwards.

In order to achieve this, I found a website that told me how each player did each week. All I had to do was create a simple webscraper in order to go through the four majors positions. This would give me every players stats for every week in one csv file.

Migrate your Kimono Projects to Portia

Heads up, Kimono Labs users!

Today, we are releasing a tool to help you migrate your Kimono projects to Portia.

All you have to do is provide your Kimono credentials for the tool and let it convert your Kimono projects into Portia projects.

You will then be able to run those projects on Scrapy Cloud or on your own Portia instance, since Portia is open source.

Stay tuned for the Portia 2.0 beta release, that will take place at Feb 29th. It is a huge milestone on Portia history.

Portia 2.0 brings a brand new user interface, based on a meticulous study led by our UX team – and of course all existing Portia spiders will continue to be supported.

It also brings some long awaited features:

  • Automatic detection of similar items in a page
  • Extraction of items with nested structure
  • A single attribute now can be assigned to multiple fields

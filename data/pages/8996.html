Python library for interacting with Avid Systems.

Currently focused on the Avid Interplay platform (tested against Avid Interplay WS >= 3.0.5). It comes bundled with a CLI to make simple calls to Avid Interplay.

Under the hood, using mostly suds-jurko and click for the CLI.

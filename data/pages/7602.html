pyyawt 0.1.1

library for wavelet calculations

This toolbox is aimed to mimic matlab wavelet toolbox. Most of the functions are
similiar to their counterparts in Matlab equivalents. pyyawt is based on Scilab Wavelet Toolbox (http://atoms.scilab.org/toolboxes/swt)

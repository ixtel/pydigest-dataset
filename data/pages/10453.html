gpiodaemon 1.2.4

Raspberry Pi GPIO setup utility for rpi2caster

This daemon runs on startup as root (via systemd service file) and sets up the inputs and outputs for the shutdown and reboot buttons and a LED for indicating the system status (ready or powering off).

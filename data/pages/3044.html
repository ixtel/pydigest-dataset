New core team members

Posted by Carl Meyer on January 11, 2015

The Django core team is pleased to announce the addition of two new members.

Josh Smeaton spent the better part of a year shepherding a major patch to the Django ORM to unify the representation of SQL expressions and allow much more flexible annotations and aggregations, and has been active in other work related to the ORM.

Markus Holtermann was the most active non-core-team code contributor to Django in 2014, with a particular focus on the new migrations system.

We thank Josh and Markus for their contributions, and look forward to their continued work to improve the Django project.

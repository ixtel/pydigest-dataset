Python is one of the most elegant and complete programming language and comes packed with tons of features to develop web applications as well as applications that run on your computer. Python is high level language, simple to learn and comes with multiplatform support.

The depth of Python makes it specifically useful in education and research sector and that is one reason for Python’s popularity among students.

Topics:  

1) Pro Developer Series: Talks from the Front Lines
We'll be sitting down one-on-one with a few special guests to discuss Agile Development, TDD, breaking into the industry, hiring, and how to succeed as a developer. Audience participation and heckling is encouraged!

2) Keynote: "Leveling Up as a Developer: RPG Edition"
Growing as a developer has plenty of similarities to leveling up in a Role-Playing Game like AD&D, Final Fantasy or World of Warcraft. In this talk, we'll discuss Levels, Stats, Quests and of course Experience Points, with the goal of getting to the next level.

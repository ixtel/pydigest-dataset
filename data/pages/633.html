    Exchange Rate

    Topics

    Articles
    OEmbed Video Parsing and Storage

============================================
QUICK START
============================================

Install django-current, south, unidecode, xlwt, django-grappelli, Pillow and ReportLab(recommended)


cd /usr/src
git clone https://github.com/nnmware/nnmware.git
cd /usr/src/nnmware
./syncdb
./runserver:8080

locate you browser at http://localhost:8080/admin/ ,
login and enjoy.

you may disable django-grappelli in settings.py

certitude 0.0.1

A library that provides access to system certificate stores.

A small library that makes the OS X keychain-based certificate store available to OpenSSL in a format that OpenSSL appreciates.

In the future, this library may expand to become a truly cross-platform tool that pastes over the differences between platforms.
